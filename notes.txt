auth microservice
business logic - check reservations and apply reservations rules
database microservice - makes all requests to database and receives requests from business logic microservice
gateway - requests go to gateway to be sent to auth microservice


user microservice 
  CreateUser(name, email, passwordHash)
• GetUserById(userId)
• UpdateUser(userId, fields)
• DeleteUser(userId) (soft delete recommended)
• ListTeamsForUser(userId)
• ListTasksAssignedToUser(userId, filters)

team microservice
• CreateTeam(name, ownerId)
• GetTeam(teamId)
• UpdateTeam(teamId, fields)
• DeleteTeam(teamId)
• AddUserToTeam(teamId, userId, role)
• RemoveUserFromTeam(teamId, userId)
• ListTeamMembers(teamId)
• ListTeamTasks(teamId, filters)

note microservice
• CreateNote(authorId, teamId, content)
• GetNote(noteId)
• UpdateNote(noteId, content)
• DeleteNote(noteId)
• ListNotesForTeam(teamId)
• ListNotesForTask(taskId) (if tasks can have multiple notes)
